1. Current Cloud Environment and Pain Points
   1.​ Primary Cost Centers: EC2, storage and Kubernetes.
   2.​ Business vs. Engineering Conflict: Business teams often demand extreme performance - reacting,
       for instance, to a one-second latency spike - without visibility into the associated compute cost.
   3.​ Cultural Hurdles: Developers focus on meeting business requirements and "getting the job done"
       rather than code optimization, and there is a general lack of clarity on who owns cost - the
       person incurring it or the person monitoring it.
   4.​ Operational Constraints: The team is small and lacks the bandwidth to manually chase dozens of
       application owners for approval on downsizing or optimization recommendations.

   3.​ Scale: Currently 550-600 employees supporting global clients including a major US grocery chain, a global apparel retailer, Banana
       Republic and a security software vendor.
   4.​ Leadership: Includes Rajan (who scaled a major tech company's India development center to 4,000 people)

    2.​ Solution: "Showback" - a dashboard that tracks individual team performance against budgets

         actually participating in cost-saving.
E. AI Integration via MCP Server
    1.​ XYZ exposes 43 tools to coding agents like GitHub Copilot via the Model Context Protocol
        (MCP).
    2.​ Use case: a developer can query their IDE ("What is the current cost of this Redis instance?") or
